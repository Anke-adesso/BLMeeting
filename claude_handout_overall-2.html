Your AI at work — what it is, what it can do, and where to start.
What it is
Claude is Anthropic's AI — built for reasoning and problem-solving. Known for strong document handling and for being more willing than most models to say "I'm not sure" rather than bluff.
How you access it
Request your Enterprise account via the matrix24 tool, then log in at claude.ai via adesso SSO. Add-ins available in Word, Outlook, Excel, and PowerPoint.
Your data
On the Enterprise plan: Anthropic does not train on your data. Conversations stay private and are not used to improve Claude models.

Good to know
What Claude doesn't do
  • No live internet by default — knowledge has a cutoff. Don't use for real-time data without web search enabled.
  • Memory is off by default — but you can enable it in your settings. It's not total recall: Claude keeps the most important context based on what you mention most. For specific things you want remembered, use the edit function to add them explicitly.
  • More likely to say "I don't know" than to bluff — but no AI is immune to confident mistakes. Verify anything that matters.
  • MCP connection to M365 not yet available at adesso. Add-ins (Word, Outlook, Excel, PowerPoint) are live.

Already using AI tools
One tool is not a strategy.
You're already using AI — that's the right instinct. The next step is knowing which tool does what well, and stopping to use one where another wins.
Where Claude wins
4 things Claude does better for consulting work
Honest when uncertain
Claude is more likely to flag what it's unsure about than to answer confidently regardless. It still makes mistakes — but for client work, a model that signals doubt beats one that's smoothly wrong.
1 million token context
Entire project folder, long legal doc, full meeting backlog — one conversation. Far less chunking and context-juggling than a small window forces.
Projects with memory
Brief it once on the engagement. It remembers. Same persona, same context — every session. Stop re-explaining the background.
Writing that sounds less like AI
Ask it to "sound less like AI wrote this" and the output noticeably improves — more calibrated, closer to your tone. Not invisible, but a real step away from generic.
Honest take
Different tools. Different strengths. Use both.
Use Claude for
Document work, long-context reasoning, client comms, anything where being wrong is expensive.
Use other AI tools for
Image generation, voice interfaces, specialised plugins, or workflows you've already built somewhere else.
No model leads all categories — and that changes monthly. Platform competence beats platform loyalty.
